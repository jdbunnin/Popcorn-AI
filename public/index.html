✓ All Three Hits Were Predictable

Demand signals were detectable 3-6 months before release in every case. Multiple search trends accelerated simultaneously, creating a measurable "demand convergence" that Popcorn AI would have flagged.

Methodology

We analyzed Google Trends search volume data for terms related to each piece of content, measuring velocity (rate of growth), acceleration (is growth speeding up), and convergence (are multiple signals growing simultaneously).

The Popcorn Score (0-100) combines velocity, convergence, and consistency of pre-release signals. A score above 75 indicates strong detectable demand. Above 50 indicates emerging demand.
